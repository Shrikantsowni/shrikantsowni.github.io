Pianos are a versatile and iconic musical instrument. They are widely used in classical, jazz, and popular music. With their broad range and expressive capabilities, pianos have been a favorite for centuries. Did you know the modern piano has over 12,000 parts, including 10,000 moving ones?

Music theory is the study of the practices and possibilities of music. It explores the elements of sound, melody, harmony, and rhythm. Learning music theory can help you understand how music works and improve your skills as a performer or composer.

Composition is the process of creating music. It involves crafting melodies, harmonies, and rhythms to produce a unique piece of music. Famous composers like Beethoven and Mozart set the foundation for modern music, and today, composition spans across various genres and styles.
